Fun facts about us!

We pick the freshest fruits straight from the farm, ensuring top-notch quality from the very roots. Our commitment to sourcing the best ingredients guarantees a burst of natural flavor in every scoop.

Founded in 1935 by Jackson in Florence, Italy, our ice creamery began with a passion for crafting authentic, delicious gelato. Over the decades, we've preserved our artisanal roots while innovating with unique flavors that delight taste buds worldwide.

Since our founding in Florence, Italy in 1935, we've expanded to 42 countries over 89 years, bringing our artisanal ice cream to Japan, Australia, the USA, and beyond. Our commitment to quality and innovation has made Jackson Ice Creamery a beloved global brand.
